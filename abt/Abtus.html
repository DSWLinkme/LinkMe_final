ABOUT US


The aim of this platform is to regularly search for, update and find bursary opportunities,
students who need financial aid and publish all the accurate information about both
parties and make it easily accessible to both parties. This platform is the point of
converging of bursaries and students.

Our main goal is to assist students find funding as most struggle to find ways to access
bursaries and/or how to reach out to them. All students are welcome the surf the platform
and seek funds. Although a strong academic record is highly recommended. Bursaries listed
are awarded to students who are currently in Matric and who will be studying at University
or College the following year, as well as current tertiary students who cannot afford to pay
their extortionate fees.
